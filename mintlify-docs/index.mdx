FinalRun is an AI-powered CLI for testing Android and iOS apps with natural language. You write tests the way you'd describe a flow to a teammate — "Open the app, tap **Sign in**, enter the email." FinalRun's AI launches your app on a real device, emulator, or simulator, executes each step, and verifies the outcome — handing you a pass/fail report with video, screenshots, and device logs. The [AI agent skills](/running/ai-agent-skills) for Claude Code, Cursor, and Codex author and fix those tests for you. See it in action below.
